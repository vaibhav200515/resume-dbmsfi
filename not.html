The NOT operator in SQL is used to reverse the result of a condition.

If a condition is TRUE, NOT makes it FALSE and vice versa.

It's helpful when you want to exclude specific values or conditions from your query result.

üìò Syntax

SELECT column1, column2, ...
FROM table_name
WHERE NOT condition;

üßæ Example: Select students who are not enrolled in BBA
